Lasagna

Delicious beef lasagna with a white creamy sauce. A family favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 14 ounce marinara sauce
  • 16 ounce lasagna noodles
  • 8 ounce ricotta cheese
  • 1 pound shredded mozzarella
  • 1 tbsp oregano
  • 1 tbsp sage
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp dried onion flakes
  • 1 pinch basil

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Stir and season with basil, oregano, sage, minced garlic, and onion flakes. Simmer for about 15 minutes, stirring frequently. Stir in the marinara sauce, and remove from the heat.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  3. Place a layer of the meat and sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover with a layer of dry noodles. Spread a thin layer of ricotta cheese over the noodles, and sprinkle with a little mozzarella cheese. Cover with another layer of sauce, and repeat layering ending with the meat and sauce on top. Reserve about 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese for later.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes in the preheated oven, but check after 30 minutes depending on how thick the lasagna is. Sprinkle the reserved cheese over the top, and return to the oven for about 10 minutes for a nice melted cheese topping.
